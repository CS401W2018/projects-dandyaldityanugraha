  1. What is the difference between block tags and inline tags.

    Block Tags: Block-level elements take up the full width of their container and start on a new line. They create "blocks" of content.

    Inline Tags: Inline-level elements only take up as much width as necessary for their content and do not force a new line.

  2. What is the margin, border, and padding?

    Margin: The space outside the border of an element. Margins create space between elements.

    Border: The space between the margin and the padding of an element. It surrounds the content and padding and can be styled (e.g., solid, dashed).

    Padding: The space inside the border of an element, between the content and the border.
