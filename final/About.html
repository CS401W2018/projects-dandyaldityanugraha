Our Story

The culture in Indonesia is that we must remove our footwear when we visit someone's home. Based on our real-life experience as a full-time missionary of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, this became a big problem that made us feel insecure about our performance. After returning home from our full-time missionary service you build Shoesiety.

About Shoesiety

Shoesiety established since 2022.
Shoesiety was founded on the true needs of everyone. With one mission: Realizing dreams and aspirations with every step taken. Effectively kills germs and odor-causing bacteria, safe for all types of shoes and your skin, and gives long-lasting freshness to your shoes.
