What is Stranger Things?

"Stranger Things" is a popular Netflix sci-fi horror series created by the Duffer Brothers. Set in the 1980s, the show takes place in Hawkins, Indiana, where a young boy, Will Byers, mysteriously disappears. Discover that his disappearance in to another dimension called the Upside Down, with dangerous creatures in it. Every seasons always get us immerse into the story and the challenges that they face.

Throughout its seasons, Stranger Things explores government conspiracies, secret experiments, and the Upside Down world. The main cast, Winona Ryder, David Harbour, Millie Bobby Brown, and Finn Wolfhard, has successful brought this series worldwide. The themes of the show are growing up, thiller, and the bond between friends can makes the watcher relates to their lives.
