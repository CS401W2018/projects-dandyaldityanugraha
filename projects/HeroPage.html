Gaten Matarazzo a.k.a Dustin Henderson

is one of the main character in the Netflix series Stranger Things. He is known for his funny personality, smart, and loyalty to his friends. Dustin is characterized by his optimistic and kind-heart. He has a strong sense of humor and he is often be the one who breaking the tense and bring the mood even in the darkest situations. Even though he got bullied by his friends at school he still show up and brave to fight back. He also remains confident and self-assured. His tootless grin, especially in the earlier seasons, become one of his iconic.

Impact on the series

Dustin's character grow stronger in every seasons. He adds a unique blend of humor, heart, and intellect to Stranger Things. His loyalty to his friends and his quick thinking in the dangerous situations make him has big impact of the group. Over the seasonsm, Dustin also become a beloved character, know for his bravery, intelligence, and ability to bring a smile to the audience is the moste impactful moments.
