Stranger Things Series on Netflix

Stranger Things?

Stranger Things is a popular Netflix series that has genre such as science fiction, horror, and nostalgia for the 1980s. Created by the Duffer Brothers, the show is set in the town of Hawkins, Indiana. On of the best show in Netflix that I have seen so far. There are 4 seasons and they will release the 5 season next year.

Fact: Now Stranger Things in #2 (second) Most searched sci-fi show on streaming today.

Brief Summary

In the Stranger Things series, the ordinary town of Hawkins is turned upside down. The series bigins with the mysterious disappearance of a young boy named Willy Byers and the discovery of a girl with supranatural powers known as El (Eleven). His friends Mike, Lucas, and Dustin set out to find him. As the story unfolds, a group of friends embarks on a thrilling adventure that involves goverment conspiracies, secret experiments and an alternate dimension known as the Upside Down world. The things that makes it more interest with the multiple storylines, including the struggles of Will's mom to find her son, and the efforts of the local police chief to uncover the thurth.
